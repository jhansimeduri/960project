PRERNA means Inspiration - PRERNA is the Annual Intercollegiate Literary & Cultural fest conducted by St. Mary’s College. The college has been conducting “PRERNA” for more than a decade now. We at St. Mary’s believe in inspiring students to develop their personality. Students of more than a hundred colleges in twin cities are invited to be associated with this inter-collegiate annual fest. It is a platform to bring students of various colleges together and give them an opportunity to kindle their spirit of competition
